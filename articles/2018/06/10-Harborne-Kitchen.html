Birmingham Spots // Harborne Kitchen

10 / 06 / 2018

The perks of living in a big city are fun things to do, events happening and plenty of restaurants to discover ...

One of them is Harborne Kitchen. Since its opening in 2016, this restaurant has become one of my favourite places to eat in Birmingham. It offers great food, great service and has a wonderfully relaxed atmosphere.

Reasonably priced for what you get, too.

Before it became Harborne Kitchen, it started off as a small-scale restaurant where you could get the best salted caramel chicken wings in town. I know, sounds strange, right? But trust me, they were chronic. Since then, the restaurant owners split in two and one continued what today is Harborne Kitchen, and the other apparently has a new place in Henley in Arden (note to self: I need to check this place out).

This is a great place to spend the evening if you fancy a finer meal, but don't want to break the bank.

Harborne Kitchen 175 - 179 High St Birmingham B17 9QE
