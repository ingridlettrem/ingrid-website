Easy Peasy Prawn Fajitas

06 / 06 / 2018

These prawn fajitas are so good, healthy and easy to make! For two people, you'll need:

  • prawns
  • fajitas
  • fajita spice mix
  • 3 peppers (red, green, yellow)

For the guacamole:

  • 1-2 avocado
  • 1 tomato, deseeded
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1/2 red chilli, deseeded
  • garlic
  • lime
  • coriander

For the salsa:

  • crisp cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 - 1 small red onion
  • 1/2 - 1 small onion
  • cumin
  • lime
  • coriander

Salt and pepper to taste.

I always start with making the guacamole and salsa, so that's out of the way. For the guacamole, deseed and chop the tomato and add it to already mashed avocado. Add finely chopped red onion and 1/2 red chilli. Lime and coriander as you wish.

For the salsa I cut crisp cherry tomatoes into quarters, add finely chopped onion. Mix together with cumin, salt, pepper, lime and coriander.

Finally, slice the peppers and fry them in some oil, add the spice mix and eventually the prawns. Adjust the cooking time depending on whether your prawns are raw or already cooked.

Dish up and enjoy!
