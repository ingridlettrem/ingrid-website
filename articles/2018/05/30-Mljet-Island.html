Mljet Island, Croatia

30 / 06 / 2018

During our time in Croatia we made sure to visit the island of Mljet. The island contains a national park which we had read about and wanted to visit. As much as I love the city, you can't beat the coutryside. We hired bikes and biked around the lakes. It took us just over an hour, that's including stopping here and there to enjoy the views and take pictures. Afterwards, we cooled down by jumping into the lake, which held a comfortable 25C and so wasn't cold at all, but rather refreshing!

Later in the evening we had just finished our meal at a restaurant when there was a power cut all over the island! We sat in darkness looking at each other, wondering what to do. Luckily we had already eaten, and being pretty relaxed people, we were in no rush to go anywhere. The waiters came running out with candles and wine on the house - it turned out to be quite romantic, actually!

Despite the power cut (the power came back not long after we'd reached our hotel in the early hours), if you're in Croatia this is a nice island to visit. Getting there is easy; simply take the ferry from Dubrovnik to Pomena. It was nice to escape the crowds of Dubrovnik. We only spent a night there but that was enough for us. Let me know if you visit!

Ingrid x
